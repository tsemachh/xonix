HOW TO PLAY

Claimed land — fill 75% to clear the level. The sea — draw across it to wall off territory. Your trail — finish it back on land to capture. Don't let anything touch it. Sea balls — bounce around the water; deadly to you & your trail. Sparx — patrol the edges of your land. Don't get cornered. The Qix — an erratic line-creature roaming the water (your main enemy). ◆Twin-Qix levels: draw a line between the two Qixes to split them apart — it clears the level instantly and doubles your score, stacking up to ×9. Power-ups — grab them on your land.
Move: touch and hold anywhere on the board — a joystick appears; drag to steer, release to stop. You can also enable tilt (⌖ button) or use arrow keys / WASD. Steer into the water to start drawing a line, and back onto land to seal off territory. Capturing a region with no enemies fills it in.
