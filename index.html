HOW TO PLAY

Claimed land — fill 75% to clear the level. The sea — draw across it to wall off territory. Your trail — finish it back on land to capture. Don't let anything touch it. Sea balls — bounce around the water; deadly to you & your trail. Sparx — patrol the edges of your land. Don't get cornered. The Qix — an erratic line-creature roaming the water. Power-ups — grab them on your land.
Move: swipe anywhere on the board, use the on-screen D-pad, or arrow keys / WASD. You keep gliding in the last direction — steer into the water to start drawing, and back onto land to seal it off. Capturing a region with no enemies fills it in.
